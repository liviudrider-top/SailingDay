# SailingDay - National Sailing Day Interactive Experience

A dark, underground-themed web application celebrating National Sailing Day with regatta events, boat registration, and interactive gameplay elements.

### Interactive Elements
- **Floating Boat Animation**: Watch sailboats drift across the hero section with realistic floating movements
- **Countdown Timer**: Real-time countdown to National Sailing Day 2025 (June 21, 2025)
- **Parallax Scrolling**: Hero section responds to scroll for immersive depth effect
- **Wave Animations**: Dynamic wave effects at the bottom of the hero section

### Game-Like Interactions
- **Registration Challenge**: Complete the registration form to "unlock" your participation in the regatta
- **Gallery Discovery**: Click on gallery items to discover different aspects of sailing competitions
- **Navigation Quest**: Smooth scroll navigation through different sections of the sailing experience
- **Social Media Hunt**: Connect with the sailing community through social links

### Achievement System
- **Registration Badge**: Successfully register for National Sailing Day
- **Explorer Badge**: Visit all sections of the website
- **Social Sailor Badge**: Connect with social media platforms
- **Regatta Expert Badge**: Learn about all different boat types and events
