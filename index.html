About National Sailing Day

National Sailing Day is a nationwide celebration of sailing, bringing together enthusiasts, beginners, and families to experience the thrill and beauty of sailing.

Join us for a day filled with sailing demonstrations, beginner lessons, regattas, and maritime festivities at locations across the country.
